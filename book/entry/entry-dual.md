# Dual Surfaces

This cluster formalizes a reality designers can no longer ignore:

**Your system is operated by humans and machines.**

## What Dual Surfaces solve

- semantic drift between UI and APIs
- unsafe automation
- invisible authority
- unexplainable behavior

Dual design enforces parity.
